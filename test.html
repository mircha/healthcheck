Overview of your Service Summary

Introduction

Welcome to your annual Masstech system health report. We are delighted to provide this report to help you get the most out of your Masstech storage and content management system.

Each year we perform a proactive check-up for every customer who has a current support agreement. This report is the result of your annual check-up.

A Masstech engineer has connected into your system, performed a series of checks, gathered some helpful data and assessed whether there might be ways to enhance how the system runs. He or she has recorded any fine-tuning recommendations and logged any issues into our issue tracking system.

The report is divided into the following sections:

  1. SYSTEM SUMMARY: a brief description of your environment and support agreement.
  2. CONTENT: An overview of the content assets you have and the growth this year.
  3. HARDWARE: A summary of the libraries and servers that are used by your Masstech system.
  4. SOFTWARE: The versions and installation history for your Masstech software.
  5. SUPPORT: An overview of the service that you have received from us this year.
  6. CONCLUSIONS: Observations and recommendations from our support engineer.

Navigating the report

Move up and down through the report for each new section. Use the keyboard. Move left and right to see each page within each section. Use the keyboard or click right/left arrows. You can open or collapse the menu with the icon on the top left of the screen.

Feedback

If you would like to clarify any of the information, or would like to discuss the findings, just let us know. Please email support@masstech.com. If you would like to see further information added to the health check, or have any more general comments, please let me know directly. I would be delighted to hear from you.
