Your System Review Report

Dear Masstech user

Welcome to your annual Masstech system health report. We are delighted to provide this report to help you get the most out of your Masstech storage and content management system. Each year we perform a proactive check-up for every customer who has a current support agreement. This report is the result of your annual check-up.

A Masstech engineer has connected into your system, performed a series of checks, gathered some helpful data and assessed whether there might be ways to enhance how the system runs. He or she has recorded any fine-tuning recommendations and logged any issues into our issue tracking system.

The report is divided into the following sections:

  1. SYSTEM SUMMARY: a brief description of your environment and support agreement.
  2. CONTENT: An overview of the content assets you have and the growth this year.
  3. HARDWARE: A summary of the libraries and servers that are used by your Masstech system.
  4. SOFTWARE: The versions and installation history for your Masstech software.
  5. SUPPORT: An overview of the service that you have received from us this year.
  6. CONCLUSIONS: Observations and recommendations from our support engineer.

Navigating the report

Move up and down through the report for each new section. Use the keyboard. Move left and right to see each page within each section. Use the keyboard or click right/left arrows. You can open or collapse the menu with the icon on the top left of the screen.

Privacy & Feedback

The data that we collect during these reviews is only used for internal purposes by Masstech, and none of your data will be shared with anybody outside of Masstech. In addition, we do not capture any details regarding actual content, such as file names. If you have any concerns or questions, please direct them to me.If you would like to see further information added to the health check, or have any more general comments, please let me know directly. I would be delighted to hear from you.

Sincerely,
Lee Sheppard, VP of customer services. Lee.sheppard@masstech.com

The system analysis was scheduled on April 7th, 2020 and was completed on April 9th, 2020.

There were no issues found while performing the system health check.  After finishing the system analysis, the engineer concluded:

I connected to the customer's system and didn't find any issues. The system is working fine, there aren't any failed workflows, transfers or transcodes. All the servers are online, the daily database backups are working. The storage locations are all connected and are discovering content.

I connected on all servers and made sure there is plenty of free space on the c: drive. Also, the cache space is between limits and there is enough space available on tape for writing in all media groups.

In conclusion, I haven't found any trace of malfunctions on the system.
